Anime has become an increasingly popular form of entertainment among young people in recent years. Anime characters often have complex backstories, unique personalities, and relatable struggles, making them more interesting and engaging for young people.

Easy accessibility: With the rise of online streaming services like Netflix, Hulu, and Crunchyroll, anime has become much more accessible to young people. Young people often share memes, fan art, and other content related to their favorite anime series, creating a sense of community and buzz around these shows.

Escapism: Anime offers young people a way to escape from reality and immerse themselves in fantastical worlds and stories.
